What Your Scores Mean

Once you've done your Read Easy test, you'll see that it'll have an overall grade colour. Your piece will also have two scores - one for Reading Ease and one for your Grade level.

Here's how to read those results:

1. The higher your Reading Ease, the easier it is to read your writing.

2. The lower your Grade level, the more people will understand your writing.

  • Green
    Good to go.
  • Yellow
    Some work to be done.
  • Red
    Way too complicated.
