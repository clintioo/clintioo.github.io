Here's a quick and easy way to make sure your words are easy to read. Just copy and paste the text you want to check into the space provided, then click "Check It" and wait for your results.

How to read your results:

The higher your Reading Ease, the easier it is to read your writing.

The lower your Grade Level, the more people will understand your writing.

  • Green
    Good to go.
  • Yellow
    Some work to be done.
  • Red
    Way too complicated.
Ideal scores: Reading ease: 70+    |    Grade level: 5-7
